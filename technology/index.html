Interface Engineering Platform

We are developing scalable technologies that engineer functionality directly at material interfaces, enabling advanced textile systems with enhanced multi-functionality. By incorporating functionality directly into the material architecture, we reduce the reliance on conventional coatings and eliminate post-treatment process. We are creating advanced performance PPE’s, industrial textiles, and next-generation functional materials.
