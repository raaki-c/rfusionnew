R-Fusion Accepted into NC Innovation Through Appalachian State University

R-Fusion has been accepted into NC Innovation through Appalachian State University after a highly competitive selection process.

This milestone marks an important step forward for R-Fusion as it continues building innovative solutions with real-world impact. Being selected for NC Innovation reflects the strength of R-Fusion’s vision, the promise of its work, and its potential to contribute meaningfully to North Carolina’s growing innovation ecosystem.

NC Innovation supports high-potential ventures emerging from North Carolina’s universities and research institutions. Through this opportunity, R-Fusion will gain access to valuable resources, mentorship, and support designed to help advance early-stage innovation from concept to market.

R-Fusion’s acceptance highlights the company’s momentum and commitment to transforming bold ideas into scalable, practical outcomes. As part of this next chapter, R-Fusion looks forward to working through Appalachian State University and engaging with the broader NC Innovation network to continue developing its work and expanding its reach.

This recognition is a meaningful step toward bringing R-Fusion’s vision to life and advancing innovation rooted in purpose, execution, and impact.
